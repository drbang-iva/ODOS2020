# Open Source OD

Open source practice management for independent optometry.

**Built by a practicing O.D. Not a tech company. Not a startup. A doctor who got tired of waiting for someone else to build what we need.**

---

## Why

Every major eyecare PMS locks independent O.D.s out of their own data. No API access. No integrations. No customization. Meanwhile, dentists have had Open Dental for 20 years.

OSOD changes that. Your data. Your hardware. Your practice. Open source, local-first, built for how optometry actually works.

## Status

**Pre-alpha.** Phase 1 (Patients + Scheduling) in development.

## Tech Stack

- **TypeScript** (full-stack)
- **React** (frontend)
- **Node.js** (backend)
- **PostgreSQL** (database)
- **Local-first** (runs on your hardware)
